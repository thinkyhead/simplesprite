A gadget in its basic form behaves visually like a button. You can load images for up to four rollover and clicked states, or use a sprite group as background and indicator. You can set callbacks for various events.

The current gadget suite has buttons, labels, sliders, and simple text fields. More complex gadgets are made by placing individual gadgets into groupings. The gadgets then broadcast to their grouping object.
