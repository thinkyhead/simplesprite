SS_SFont.h

SS_EditString

SuperClass: SS_String

This class implements the display and behaviors of an editable string. This includes implementing the selection of characters, the replacement of the selected characters, the rendering of the selection in focused and unfocused states, and hooks for keyboard and mouse interaction.
