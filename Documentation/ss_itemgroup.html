SS_ItemGroup.h

SS_ItemGroup

Superclasses: SS_LayerItem, SS_ItemList

Item groups are containers that group LayerItems together into a single manipulable unit. This allows visual objects to be built out of pieces which have independent articulation. For example, you could make a space ship with a hull, engines, turrets, and wings as Sprites and then combine them into a group. Parts of the ship such as the turrets can then move and rotate independently of the whole ship. An item group can contain any combination of sprites, vector objects, and even other groups in the same group.

This ability for groups to contain other groups means there's no limit to the complex kinematics you can create.

A group combines the collisions of its members into a single collision mask for the purposes of reporting collisions.
